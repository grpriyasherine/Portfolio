## Overview

WhatsApp Status lets you share temporary updates with your contacts.
Post text, photos, videos, music, voice messages, or AI-generated images.
You can see who viewed each of your statuses.

Use status to share daily moments, express yourself, or stay connected.
This guide teaches you to add statuses on mobile and desktop devices.
Follow the steps below to choose your status type and platform.

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Key Features:</span>
- Temporary updates (24-hour visibility)
- Multiple media types (text, photos, videos, music, voice, AI images)
- Privacy controls for who can view
- View tracking for your statuses
- Cross-platform support (mobile and desktop)

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">When to Use:</span>
- Share daily moments or quick updates
- Express creativity with media or AI-generated content
- Stay connected without formal messaging
- Broadcast temporary information like events or availability

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Limitations:</span>
- Music, Voice, and AI Image statuses are mobile-only
- Desktop supports Text and Photos & Videos only
- Feature availability may vary by region (e.g., Meta AI in select countries)

## Prerequisites

Ensure you meet the following requirements before you add a status update:

- Update WhatsApp to the latest version on your device.
- Log in to your WhatsApp account with a verified phone number.
- Ensure your device has an internet connection.
- Enable WhatsApp microphone permission on your device (required for Voice status only).

**Feature Availability Note:** Some features like Music, Voice, and AI Images may not be available in all regions. Check WhatsApp settings or app store for updates. Meta AI availability varies by country.

## Update Your WhatsApp Status on Mobile

To add a WhatsApp status update on mobile,

1. On your Android device, open **WhatsApp**.
2. On the WhatsApp home screen, tap the **Updates** tab.
3. On the **Updates** screen, tap **Add status**.
4. On the **Add status** screen, select one of the following status types:

---

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Text Status</span>

To add a text status,

a. Tap **Text**.\
b. In the **Type a status** field, type your message.

> 💡 **Note:** Tap **Aa** to change font style, or tap the color palette to change background color.

---

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Photos & Videos Status</span>

To add a photo or video status,

a. Tap **Photos & Videos**.\
b. From your device gallery, select a photo or video.\
c. In the caption field, type a caption. *(Optional)*

> ⏱️ **Note:** Max duration: 90 seconds.

---

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Music Status</span>

To add a music status,

a. Tap **Music**.\
b. In the **Search moods** search bar, search for a track. Alternatively, browse the **Suggested**, **Mood**, or **Genre** tabs.\
c. Select the track you want to share.

---

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Voice Status</span>

To add a voice status,

a. Tap **Voice**.\
b. Press and hold the **microphone** button to record your message.\
c. Release the **microphone** button to stop recording.

> 🎤 **Note:** Enable microphone permission: **Settings** > **Apps** > **WhatsApp** > **Permissions** > **Microphone**.

---

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">AI Image Status</span>

To add an AI image status,

a. Tap **AI images**.\
b. In the **Describe an image** field, type a description of the image that you want to generate.\
c. From the generated results displayed on the screen, select an image.\
d. Optionally, edit with stickers or effects before posting.

Tap **Send** to post your status.

**Quick Command Path (Android):** WhatsApp > Updates tab > Add status > Select type > Customize > Send

## Update Your WhatsApp Status on Desktop

On desktop, WhatsApp Web and the WhatsApp Desktop app support Text and Photos & Videos status updates. Music, Voice, and AI Image statuses are available on mobile only.

To add a WhatsApp status update on desktop,

1. Open **WhatsApp Web** in your browser, or open the **WhatsApp Desktop** app on your computer.
2. In the left panel, click the **Status** tab.
   ![Desktop Status Tab](Images/whatsapp-status-desktop-status.png)
3. On the **Status** screen, click **Add status (+)**.
4. On the **Add status** screen, select one of the following status types:

---
<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Text Status</span>

To add a text status,

a. Click **Text**.\
b. In the text field, type your message.



> 💡 **Note:** Use the formatting toolbar to change font style or background color.

Tap **Send** to post your status.

---

<span style="color: #6b6b6b; font-weight: bold;">Photos & Videos Status</span>

To add a photo or video status,

a. Click **Photos & Videos**.\
b. In the file browser, navigate to and select a photo or video file from your computer.\
c. In the caption field, type a caption. *(Optional)*

Tap **Send** to post your status.

**Quick Command Path (Desktop):** WhatsApp Web/Desktop > Status tab > Add status (+) > Select type > Customize > Send
## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Can I add multiple status updates at once?**

**Ans.** Yes. Contacts see all active statuses as a sequence in the order you post them.

**Q2. Can I control who sees my status?**

**Ans.** Yes. Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **Privacy** > **Status** to set visibility options.

**Q3. Can I delete a status before it expires?**

**Ans.** Yes. On the **Updates** tab, hold your status, then tap **Delete**. Deleted statuses cannot be recovered.

**Q4. Can I save a status before it expires?**

**Ans.** Yes. On the **Updates** tab, open your status and tap the **Download** icon to save it to your device gallery.

## Troubleshooting

The following table describes common WhatsApp Status issues and how to resolve them.

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|---|---|---|
| **Add status** option does not appear on the Updates tab | WhatsApp needs an update or the feature is unavailable in your region | Update WhatsApp. If issue persists, contact WhatsApp Support. |
| Status posts but contacts cannot see it | Privacy settings exclude the contact, or the contact uses an older WhatsApp version | Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Status** to confirm access. Ask contact to update WhatsApp. |
| Music option does not appear on the Add status screen | The Music feature is not yet available in your region | Update WhatsApp. Music availability varies by region. |
| AI images option does not appear | Meta AI is not yet available in your region, or your WhatsApp version requires an update | Update WhatsApp. Meta AI availability varies by region. |
| Voice status has no sound on playback | Microphone permission was not enabled during recording | Go to **Settings** > **Apps** > **WhatsApp** > **Permissions** > **Microphone** and enable permission. Re-record the voice status. |
| Status does not post — shows an error | Unstable internet connection | Connect to a stable Wi-Fi network or improve mobile data signal strength, then retry. |


**For issues not listed above (general troubleshooting):**
- Check app permissions: [WhatsApp permissions (Android/iOS)](https://faq.whatsapp.com/ios/23558736/).
- Clear WhatsApp cache (Android): [WhatsApp cache cleanup](https://faq.whatsapp.com/android/26000021/).
- Reinstall app: [Install or reinstall WhatsApp](https://faq.whatsapp.com/375965147791606/?cms_id=51774990).

- Keep text status messages short and clear.
- Enable Do Not Disturb before recording a voice status.
- Use specific descriptions for AI images to get more accurate results.
- Experiment with different status types to engage your contacts.
- Review your status after posting to ensure it displays correctly.
